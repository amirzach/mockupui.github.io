Author Title
1. ERWIN HUDA ZULKEFLEEZ RELATED BUDGET 2024 DIAGRAM USING FACILE PACKING TECHNIQUE
Abstract:

The project aims to achieve the issue of challenging presentation of Malaysia's budget data by proposing the packing diagram of budget visualization for better understanding spending distributions in the Malaysian Government's annual planning project. The present method involves a proper pack of effective budget data visualization that would serve to enhance the understandability of the system, thereby allowing impact of data visualization and to establish relationship between Government and public when showing impact of data in specific events. The project is designed to tackle multiple issues in the system, that would include data visualization, structure, functions and overall efficiency. The goal is to create a simple user interface that effectively displays structured data representations. The project is going to review and analyze different visualization studies, taking into consideration the Malaysia budget 2024 facile worksheet and diagram in a more diverse flow.
