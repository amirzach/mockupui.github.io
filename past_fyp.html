SMART INVENTORY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM IR VISUALIZATION

Author: NURUL YUSOF HAZINA

Abstract:

This project focuses on developing a smart inventory management system with integrated visualization capabilities. The system leverages infrared (IR) technology to track inventory levels in real-time, enabling businesses to monitor stock levels efficiently. The visualization component provides intuitive graphical representations of inventory data, helping management make informed decisions about restocking, identifying slow-moving items, and optimizing warehouse space. The system includes features such as automated alerts for low stock, predictive analytics for demand forecasting, and customizable reporting tools. Implementation results show significant improvements in inventory accuracy and reduced operational costs for the test organizations.
